Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Simple Rent Receipt ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Tenant information (names, contact numbers, email addresses, physical addresses)
  • Receipt details (amounts, dates, payment methods, notes)
  • Payment information when using Google Pay (transaction data is processed by Google)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data
  • IP address and other network information

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our App functionality
  • Generate rent receipts based on the information you provide
  • Improve and optimize our App
  • Process payments through Google Pay
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage

Your tenant and receipt data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers. This information remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may collect and process data about you, including your device information and app usage. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Pay: When you use Google Pay for transactions in our App, your payment information is processed by Google. We do not store your payment details. Google's processing of your information is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete the information we have on you
  • Restrict or object to certain processing of your data
  • Request portability of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
